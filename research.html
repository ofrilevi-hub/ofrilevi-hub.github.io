Research

Antifungal Drug Resistance

We investigate how pathogenic fungi evolve and acquire resistance to antifungal therapies, identifying genetic mutations and pathways that enable survival against treatments.

Stress Response Pathways

Our lab studies how fungal cells respond to stress (such as oxidative or osmotic stress), uncovering how these organisms adapt to hostile environments and host defenses.

Functional Genomics Screens

Using CRISPR/Cas9 and CRISPRi techniques, we perform genome-wide screens to identify genes involved in fungal drug resistance and pathogenicity, paving the way for novel antifungal targets.
