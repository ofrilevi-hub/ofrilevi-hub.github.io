Opportunities

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers to join our lab. Current openings and ways to get involved are listed below:

  • M.Sc. Thesis Projects: Tel-Hai Biotechnology M.Sc. students interested in doing their thesis in our lab are encouraged to get in touch.
