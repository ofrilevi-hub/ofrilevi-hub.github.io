Fungal resistance mechanisms and antifungal discovery

The Levi Lab is being established at Tel-Hai University (Kiryat Shmona). Our research focuses on understanding how pathogenic fungi adapt to antifungal stress at the genetic, regulatory, and systems levels.

We integrate genome-scale CRISPRi screening, mechanistic microbiology, plant-derived antifungal discovery, and multi-omics approaches to identify pathways governing resistance and tolerance, and to enable the development of new antifungal strategies.
