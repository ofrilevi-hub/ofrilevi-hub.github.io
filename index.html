Fungal resistance mechanisms and antifungal discovery

The Levi Lab is being established at Tel-Hai University (Kiryat Shmona). We study how pathogenic fungi adapt to antifungal stress using functional genomics and multi-omics, with an emphasis on CRISPRi screens and plant-derived antifungal compounds.
