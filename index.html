About Our Research

Our lab focuses on understanding the mechanisms of fungal resistance to antifungal compounds and developing new strategies to overcome these challenges. We integrate molecular biology, genomics, and bioinformatics to explore how fungi adapt and how we can counteract their defense mechanisms.

Based at Tel-Hai Academic College in the Upper Galilee, we are committed to advancing science and training the next generation of researchers in our region.
