Levi Lab

Fungal resistance mechanisms & antifungal discovery

We study how pathogenic fungi adapt to antifungal stress in clinically and agriculturally relevant environments. Our research integrates genome-scale CRISPRi screening, plant-derived antifungal discovery, and quantitative phenotyping.
