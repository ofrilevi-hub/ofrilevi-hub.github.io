Levi Lab

Fungal resistance mechanisms and antifungal discovery

The Levi Lab studies how pathogenic fungi adapt to antifungal stress under clinically and agriculturally relevant environments, integrating genome-scale CRISPRi screening, plant-derived antifungal discovery, and quantitative phenotyping.
